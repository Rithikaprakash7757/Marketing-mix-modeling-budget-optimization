Marketing Mix Modeling & Budget Optimization
Objective

To quantify the impact of marketing channels on revenue and determine optimal budget allocation under revenue and profit objectives.

Dataset

The dataset is synthetically generated to simulate realistic multi-channel marketing spend and revenue patterns.

Due to confidentiality constraints of real-world MMM data, this project focuses on demonstrating modeling methodology and optimization logic.

Methodology

Adstock transformation to capture carryover effects

Linear regression for channel impact estimation

Elasticity and marginal ROI calculation

Constrained optimization using scipy.optimize

Revenue vs Profit objective comparison
